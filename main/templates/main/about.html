About the site and the Developer

I am Bayajit Mohammed, and I am a beginner level web developer who is trying to be the best in the field. This is the first project that I build from complete scratch. It's a dummy web application for sharing people's thoughts and discussing those thoughts with others. It's more like a Facebook post system or twitter tweets. I first thought to completely replicate one of the big social media, but since this project is for one of my courses that I am doing online I didn't have to go too overboard with it and they wanted a simple app. Besides I am still learning about web and programming so making the perfect app was still difficult for me and I hope to make better app in future through my experiences. Making this web application was painful and challenging for me (I am sure experienced would find it easy) that I was actually having fun. I also learned a ton of thing while making this app about web and honestly sometimes it blew my mind that how complex the web is. 'So the internet was not a magical thing that came out of satellite in the space, huh?!' was the first thing I was surprised about. Yeah, I know it was pretty dumb but there was so much wrong perception about technology I had that was debunked while learning and making this app. Overall I had a great experience building it, I hope to grow, learn more, and evolve.
